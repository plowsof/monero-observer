Bistroo[^1] has added[^2] XMR support to their food ordering iOS/Android app[^3]:

> Pay for your food and goodies orders with #XMR with the merchants who have enabled the crypto-payments!

The delivery service is currently available in Amsterdam, Eindhoven, Rotterdam and other cities in The Netherlands[^4].

The Bistroo app has a 4.5/5 *TrustScore* over 100+ reviews on Trust Pilot[^5].
