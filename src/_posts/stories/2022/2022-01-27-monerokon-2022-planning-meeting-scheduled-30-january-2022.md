The next *MoneroKon 2022 Planning Meeting*[^1] is scheduled to take place on Sunday, January 30th 2022 at 18:00 UTC.

The meeting should take place in the *#monero-events*[^2] Matrix/IRC channels.

## Agenda

- Update on CCS status
- Update on contract between DR and G+H (all)
- Update on availability/quotes (G+H)
- Update on website and ticketing (DR+ajs)
- Updates from kanban board (if applicable)

To view the full list of discussion topics and propose agenda items, consult issue#656[^3] from the monero-project/meta Github repo.
